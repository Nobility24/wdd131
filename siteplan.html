Echoes From The Road

Site Name

Echoes From The Road

Why this name? "Echoes" represents the lingering impact of stories and interactions. "From The Road" shows how even short conversations or experiences during a trip can leave a lasting memory, even after the ride is over.

The content will be organized into the following sections:

  • Home
  • Stories
  • About
  • Contact

Site Purpose

Echoes From The Road will serve as a digital storytelling platform featuring:

  • Curated rewritten narratives highlighting real-life stories from my time as an Uber driver as recorded on my Facebook timeline in 2019
  • Stories reimagined to offer moral lessons, cultural insights, and human connection experiences
  • Organized sections: Stories from the Road, Fictional Stories, and Folk Stories

Color Schema

The color palette is inspired by the road - twilight drives, city lights, and the warmth of human connection.

  • Midnight Blue for the headings, Navigation and Footer.
  • Cloud White for the background and content areas.
  • Amber Gold for color-box accent.

Typography

The typography combines a traditional serif for storytelling with a clean sans-serif for readability.

Headings: Playfair Display, Serif and Body Text: Roboto, Sans-serif

Visitor Scenarios

These scenarios represent the questions our target audience might have when visiting the site:

Scenario 1: The Story Seeker

"I love reading personal stories that reveal something about the human condition. Can I find meaningful, well-written narratives here that make me reflect on my own life experiences?"

Content Solution: Feature compelling story excerpts on the stories page, organized in a calendar-like format for a daily reading experience with clear themes.

Scenario 2: The Former Passenger

"I used to ride with Uber frequently and always enjoyed conversations with drivers. Does this site capture those unique, fleeting connections we make with strangers?"

Content Solution: Include a "Passenger Stories" section, author bio highlighting Uber experience, and photos that evoke the in-car experience.
